02 · Supplementary Note

Research context

The three works above anchor the proposed dissertation's conceptual foundation. Panteli et al. [1] establish the resilience trapezoid metric framework adopted in the performance quantification layer. Farrokhabadi et al. [2] provide the definitional taxonomy of microgrid stability modes — frequency, voltage, and small-signal — directly informing the hierarchical control design. Khodaei [3] demonstrates scenario-based optimal scheduling under uncertainty, the methodological antecedent for the autonomous islanding decision framework proposed in the application.
