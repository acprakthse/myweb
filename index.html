I'm an electrical and sustainable energy engineer with over a decade of hands-on experience in utility SCADA, power distribution planning, and telecommunications across Indonesia's Riau and Riau Islands provinces. My work sits at the intersection of operational engineering and research — I've led control-centre rollouts and radio communication networks, and I now spend my days writing MILP optimisation models in Python, simulating rooftop PV systems, and evaluating battery storage economics under Nordic electricity markets.

Outside the lab, I'm an avid traveller, hiker, and coffee enthusiast — I've chased 100% Arabica beans through Sumatra, and Norway and Switzerland hold a special place in my travel notebook.

Master's Thesis · 2025
Sustainable Energy System Design for Oskarshamn Harbour

A techno-economic study for Smålandshamnar AB combining electrical demand characterisation, rooftop PV simulation, and battery energy storage dispatch optimisation. Built a MILP-based dispatch model in Python/Pyomo, compared LFP, SIB, and VRFB battery configurations using PNNL cost decomposition, and applied a snow-loss model to HelioScope PV yields. Findings: standalone PV is financially feasible with a ~9-year payback; PV+BESS is not yet viable under SE3 spot prices.
